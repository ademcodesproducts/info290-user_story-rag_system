### What went well
* Global rollout of **Unity Catalog** is complete across all regions (US, EU, AP).
* **Delta Sharing** is now being used to provide clean datasets to our external partners without moving the data.
* **System Tables** are providing excellent visibility into our "Top 10 Most Expensive Queries."

### What didn't go well
* Cross-region **Unity Catalog** replication is slow; there is a significant lag between schema updates in US-East and EU-West.
* **Databricks Workflows** "Task Orchestration" is getting messy; we have a "God Job" with 25+ dependencies that is a single point of failure.
* **Vacuum** commands were run prematurely on a critical table, making it impossible to perform a `RESTORE` after a buggy update.
* The **Jobs UI** becomes sluggish when trying to view the run history of a task that runs every 5 minutes.

### Action items
* Break the "God Job" into smaller, modular Workflows using the `run_job` task type (Chris B., Nov 10).
* Update the Data Retention Policy to ensure `VACUUM` only purges files older than 30 days (Maria G., Nov 15).
* Investigate the "UC-to-UC" sharing feature for better cross-region metadata sync (Maria G., Nov 20).
