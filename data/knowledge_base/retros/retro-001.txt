### What went well
* Successfully migrated the marketing workspace to **Unity Catalog**, enabling fine-grained access control.
* Reduced idle costs by 15% after implementing custom **Cluster Policies** that enforce auto-termination after 20 minutes.
* The **Databricks Terraform provider** worked seamlessly for the new staging environment rollout.

### What didn't go well
* **DBSQL Warehouse** startup times are still hitting 2-3 minutes for the "Pro" tier, causing friction for the Tableau users who expect instant connectivity.
* Experienced several "Concurrent Append Exceptions" on our bronze tables because multiple **Auto Loader** streams were writing to the same Delta path without proper partitioning.
* **DBU consumption** spiked unexpectedly over the weekend because a user left a High Concurrency cluster running with no auto-stop enabled (policy bypass).
* The **Jobs API** rate limits throttled our CI/CD pipeline during the massive Friday deployment, causing several build failures.

### Action items
* Audit all cluster policies to ensure "Auto-termination" is a fixed attribute (Marcus L., Jan 22).
* Investigate **Serverless SQL Warehouses** to eliminate "cold start" latency for BI users (Priya R., Jan 25).
* Refactor Auto Loader logic to use specific checkpoints per stream (Kevin T., Jan 30).
