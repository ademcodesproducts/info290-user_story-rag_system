### What went well
* Using **MLflow Model Serving** for our real-time recommendation engine; latency is under 50ms.
* The **Feature Store** (Unity Catalog integrated) has made features discoverable across three different pods.
* The **Mosaic AI Model Training** platform allowed us to fine-tune Llama-3 with our proprietary docs in record time.

### What didn't go well
* **GPU availability** in the `us-east-1` region is a constant battle; jobs keep getting stuck in "Pending" for 30+ minutes.
* We're hitting the **MLflow experiment limit** in the workspace, making it hard to find historical runs for the churn model.
* **Vector Search** indexing is slow to update when the underlying Delta table changes; there's a noticeable lag in RAG response freshness.
* Notebook-based training is hard to unit test; we need to move towards **Databricks Asset Bundles (DABs)**.

### Action items
* Set up "Spot Instance" fallback for non-critical ML training tasks (Bella L., June 20).
* Migrate the Recommendation Engine project to Databricks Asset Bundles (Carlos D., June 30).
* Investigate "Triggered" vs "Continuous" Vector Search sync modes (Diane H., July 5).
