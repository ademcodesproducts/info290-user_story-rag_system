PAIN POINTS RAISED:

1. Priya: "Cost visibility keeps coming up in every account review. We're hearing it from $500K customers and $2M customers alike. The ask is consistent: per-team cost breakdown without custom ETL. It's been on the radar for two quarters. We need to move it up the stack."

2. Daniel: "Mosaic AI adoption is good but the feedback is that prompt versioning is the biggest missing feature. Users are iterating on prompts with no audit trail. Three enterprise customers in the last 6 weeks have mentioned that they can't use Playground in production workflows because there's no way to pin a specific prompt version."

3. Ananya: "The Feature Store data leakage incident at the insurance company (INT-006) is a pattern, not a one-off. I've now heard a similar story from two other accounts. Point-in-time join validation needs to be on the ML roadmap. The reputational risk from a leaked model going to production is significant."

DECISIONS:
- Cost attribution feature to be prioritized for H2 planning — Priya to draft PRD by end of month.
- Mosaic AI prompt versioning to be scoped as a Q4 feature — Daniel owns.
- Feature Store temporal validation added to ML Platform backlog as High priority.

OPEN QUESTION:
Are the multi-workspace admin pain points (TKT-015, INT-011, INT-013) large enough to justify a dedicated Enterprise Admin workstream in Q4? Marcus to quantify affected ACV before next sync.
