### What went well
* Implemented **Budget Policies** which successfully alerted us before we hit our quarterly DBU limit.
* **Serverless SQL** adoption has reached 60%, reducing our management overhead for clusters significantly.
* The **Unity Catalog** lineage UI has saved us hours during the recent GDPR audit.

### What didn't go well
* **IP Access Lists** are difficult to maintain as our remote workforce grows; we keep locking out legitimate users.
* The **Account Console** doesn't provide granular enough cost attribution for shared "General Purpose" clusters.
* **Databricks Workflows** job history only goes back 60 days in the UI, making year-over-year performance comparisons difficult.
* Cross-workspace **Delta Sharing** setup was confusing; the recipient token management feels very manual.

### Action items
* Develop a custom script to export Job Run metadata to a long-term Delta table for reporting (Steve M., Aug 1).
* Automate IP Access List updates via a CI/CD pipeline and a central registry (Gary B., Aug 10).
* Set up a tagging enforcement policy to improve DBU cost attribution (Fiona Q., Aug 15).
