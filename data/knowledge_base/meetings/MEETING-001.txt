USER PAIN POINTS RAISED:

Marcus: "Last week we had a DLT pipeline failure and the only thing in the event log was 'unexpected runtime error.' I had to open a ticket and wait 6 hours to find out it was a metastore blip. In that 6 hours our silver tables were stale and 4 analysts were blocked. The error message is the problem — if it had said 'metastore connectivity failure, retry in 10 minutes,' I would have been fine on my own."

Fatima: "We're also really constrained by the lack of task-level retry in Workflows. Our DAG has a 45-minute upstream task. If the downstream task fails on a transient error, the job retries from the start and we re-burn 45 minutes every retry. That's been a blocker for us setting more aggressive retry policies."

DECISIONS:
- Priya to add DLT error diagnostics to the H1 roadmap discovery queue.
- Anjali to share the product feedback portal link — Marcus to submit formal feature request for task-level retry.
